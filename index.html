Pricing Performances Why us?

We're "Made in Hell" from ROMANIA and we prefer our music loud and heavy!

We sing wherever you crazy metalheads want us to sing!

And especially if you have beer! Just call us!

Good Experience

We played in most of the metal festivals on the country

Low pricing

We don't care too much about the money, the pricing is low. But you have to give us as much beer as possible!

Merchandise as bonus

If you call us to play in your town we'll come with a large variety of t-shirts with the band's logo and CD's with our music. All just to sell them at cheap prices to you big fans out there!

Wanna hear a bit of how our music sounds like? Slayer is our biggest influence!
